User Experience (UX)

  • Design & Research: CollegePro

    (11/2018) CollegePro was an Interaction Design group project. We conducted surveys for students to provide insight in designing and prototyping a website, CollegePro, that aims to centralize information resources regarding academic advising, career, and research in a personalized way and allows students to communicate with others.

    Prototype Link: InVision Prototype

  • Analysis: Xbox Live Messaging Feature

    (9/2018) My first go at UX analysis for an existing system. I examined users of the Xbox Live (messaging system) and performed a simple cognitive walkthrough and user test using video recording and note taking.

  • Analysis: Music for Stories Observation

    (2/2019) The beginning observation study for my group's Music for Stories ongoing project. Our project is about the process content creators go through to determine what music and additional auditory material to add to their material. I took notes and recorded (audio & video) a participant editing a short 1-minute video.
    Includes: time-stamped field notes, AEIOU analysis, affinity diagramming, interview transcription

  • Analysis: Coffee Shop Observation

    (1/2019) My first go at non-participant observation. I observed a local coffee shop and their process for handling orders. I used affinity diagramming to analyze my time-stamped field notes and created a sequence model of the overall process.
    Includes: time-stamped field notes, AEIOU analysis, affinity diagramming, sequence model
