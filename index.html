I specialize in User Experience (UX) Research + Design.

I analyze and synthesize qualitative and quantitative data derived from human-centered research processes to design for various contexts.

I'm a remote M.S. Human-Computer Interaction student at DePaul University, and a member of the Alternative Learning Technologies and Games (ALT Games) Lab at UC Santa Cruz.
I received my B.A. Digital Media Studies from University of Rochester.

I have particular research interests in games, player experience (PX), emerging technologies, and learning.

I'm from the beautiful Northern Mariana Islands. Proud to be one of the recipients of the Gates Millennium Scholarship.
