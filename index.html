I'm a UX Researcher.

I empathize with people to better their engagement, enjoyment, and learning with technology and digital media, especially in the form of games and other immersive experiences.

I combine my analytical & creative skills to understand both the user/player experience and the iterative research and design processes to make them enjoyable and intuitive.

I have an M.S. Human-Computer Interaction with a background in digital media studies. I'm also member of the Alternative Learning Technologies and Games (ALT Games) Lab at UC Santa Cruz.

I'm interested in research related to games, player experience, XR (Mixed (MR), Augmented (AR), and Virtual (VR) realities), and learning/education.

I'm from the beautiful Northern Mariana Islands. Proud to be one of the recipients of the Gates Millennium Scholarship.
