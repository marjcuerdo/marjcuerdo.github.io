Hi, I'm Marj! (pronouns: she/her).

I'm a researcher and a second-year Computational Media PhD student in the Alternative Learning Technologies and Games (ALT Games) Lab at UC Santa Cruz.

I enjoy using analytical and creative methods to research my various interests across human-computer interaction (HCI) and games.

I'm primarily curious how games can foster affective learning through collaboration, embodied interaction [XR], and challenge design.

I also explore research areas such as player experience, games user research (GUR), and accessibility.

I believe games are a powerful medium to explore emotions and learn. Games can be used to tackle important conversations about systemic social issues such as mental health and disability stigma.
