Biography

I am an M.S. in Human-Computer Interaction student at Rochester Institute of Technology. I am particularly interested in games user research and accessibility. I completed my B.A. Digital Media Studies with minors in Computer Science and Audio and Music Engineering at University of Rochester. I am from the beautiful island of Saipan, Northern Mariana Islands. I am currently residing in snowy Rochester, New York. Proud cat mom and one of the recipients of the Gates Millennium Scholarship.
